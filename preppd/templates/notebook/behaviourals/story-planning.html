Your behavioural prep

You can use this place to prepare your "behavioural stories." If you haven't done so yet, we would recommend reviewing the behavioural component of the Getting Started guide before using this page.
When you're ready, click on one of the question types (left) and write up to three stories per type using the CARL method. You won't need more than 3 - promise! And remember: CARL stands for Context, Action, Result, and Learning. Go ahead and try writing your bullet-points below along these dimensions, and, we'll take care of storing it for you so you can come back and keep editing as you'd like.
