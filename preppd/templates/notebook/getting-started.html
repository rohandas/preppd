Getting Started

Welcome to Prepp'd!

The Getting Started section has been written to bring everything you need to know into one place. Unlike the undesirable path many before you have walked down to get ready for interviews, you don't need to go hunting to torrent hundred-plus page instructional case books only to find out their answers are at best mediocre and at worst wrong; you don't need to piece together mental math practice from a different website than where your behvaioural stories are; and you don't need to wade through troves of conflicting, static, untailored content to determine why different sources have conflicting information about recruiting processes. Prepp'd has done the work for you, with this section acting as a culmination of everything you need to know. Full stop. Plus, we've included some tips and tricks that we wish we knew at the time, that we learned through our own stumbling or as we now interview candidates from the other side of the table, all with the intent of giving you a leg up.

As you'll see below, as you work through these sections we'll help you track your progress so you can pick up where you leave off. That said, do feel free to refer back to these as you work through the interactive components of Prepp'd like the interactive Case Prep and Behavioural Prep modules.
