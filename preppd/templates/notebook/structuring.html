Case Structuring
An American entrepreneur is considering launching a new transportation startup called the Uberloop. Uberloop is a proposed mode of passenger and freight transportation that would propel a pod-like vehicle through a reduced-pressure tube that would exceed speeds achieved by regular airplanes. The pods would accelerate to cruising speed gradually using an electric motor and glide above the track using magnetic levitation or air bearings. The tubes could go above ground on columns or underground. Initial plans indicate that such an idea would not come to fruition for 10-15 years and the entrepreneur is OK with that timeline, but has brought us in to figure out one key thing: What should he consider in deciding whether or not to invest his time and money into the launch of such a product?
